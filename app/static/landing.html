Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about PlagiarismGuard

How does PlagiarismGuard detect plagiarism?
PlagiarismGuard uses a multi-agent AI system that cross-references your text against 250M+ scholarly sources and web pages. It combines web search matching, deep semantic analysis using sentence embeddings, and AI-generated text detection to identify both direct copying and paraphrased content.
Can PlagiarismGuard detect AI-generated text?
Yes. PlagiarismGuard includes a dedicated AI detection engine that identifies passages likely generated by AI tools like ChatGPT, helping you maintain an authentic academic voice in your writing.
Is PlagiarismGuard free to use?
Yes, the Basic plan is free forever with 3 tool uses per day. New users also get a free 3-day trial of the Research plan with unlimited scans — no credit card required. Paid plans start at ₹299/month.
What types of documents does it support?
PlagiarismGuard supports theses, dissertations, research papers, journal submissions, literature reviews, and any academic writing. You can paste text directly or upload PDF, DOCX, and LaTeX files for analysis.
How fast is the plagiarism analysis?
Most analyses complete in under 30 seconds. The system runs multiple AI agents in parallel — web search, semantic analysis, and AI detection — to deliver comprehensive results quickly.
